Fried Chilli Garlic Mushrooms

Detailed steps to parepare delicous fried Mushroom

Fried mushrooom is first choice when weather is rainy or you have craving for spicy and crunchy snaks.I am addicted to this spicy and easy to cook recipy, I'm sure once you make it you will crave for it again. Sharing ingredients list and cooking steps to prepare dish.

Ingredients

    Button mushroom
    Green Chilli
    Ginger
    Garlic
    Butter

Steps to be followed:

    Wash mushrooms with running water. Make sure musrooms are clean.
    Cut mushrooms in pieces, peel the garlic and crush Ginger and green chillies
    Take butter in pan and heat
    Put crushed chillies and garlic in butter
    Fry for 1-2 minutes and make sure it not deep fried
    Mix mushrooms with fried ingredients
    Cook for 5 mintues on slow flame
    Now once cooked roast on high falme for 1-2 minutes

Aha! Ready to eat
