Tahri the veg biryani

Detailed steps to parepare delicous Veg Biryani

Tahri is first choice when weather is cold or you have craving for spicy and healthy food.I am addicted to this healthy and easy to cook recipy, I'm sure once you make it you will crave for it again. Sharing ingredients list and cooking steps to prepare dish.

Ingredients

    Green vegetables like green chilli, Round gaurd, Potato, tomato, Ridge Gaurd, musrooms and anything you like to add
    Garlic, Onion
    Indian Masala ingredients
    Mustard Oil
    Curd

Steps to be followed:

    Wash vegetables with running water. Make sure veggies are clean.
    Cut all vegetables
    Take oil in cooker and heat
    Put garlic and Onion in oil and fry
    Fry for 1-2 minutes and make sure it not deep fried
    Mix masala with fried ingredients and coook for 4-5 minutes till masala starts smelling good
    Cook for 5 mintues on slow flame
    Now mix veggies in masala and keep stirring on mid falme for 3-4 minutes
    Now pour sufficient water and keep in mind green veggies also have water so don't add excess water in rice
    Wait till cooker makes pressure and whistles

Aha! Ready to eat
